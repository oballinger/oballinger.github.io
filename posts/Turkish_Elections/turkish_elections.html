Turkish Elections Data

The tool below visualizes 2,975,843 ballot-box level election results in Turkey spanning over 10 years and 20 elections. The Turkish Government publishes extraordinarily detailed electoral data. Several websites already visualize electoral data, but none go below the district level. I scraped the Turkish Elections Board website for these ballot-box level results and geocoded them to create the following visualization.
